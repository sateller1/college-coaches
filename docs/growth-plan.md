# CollegeCoaches.us 90-Day Growth Plan

## Goal
Grow qualified traffic (athletes, parents, club coaches), increase click-through rates from search, and improve monetization through ad revenue plus early sponsor and affiliate opportunities.

## Core Targets
- Increase organic clicks week-over-week.
- Improve CTR on high-impression pages.
- Expand indexable page count through static division pages and resource content.
- Build an email list for repeat traffic.
- Raise session RPM on highest-traffic pages.

---

## Phase 1 (Weeks 1-2): Tracking and Foundation

### Setup
- Confirm Google Search Console is connected and sitemap is submitted.
- Confirm Google Analytics 4 is installed and collecting events.
- Track these KPIs weekly:
  - Organic clicks
  - Impressions
  - CTR
  - Top landing pages
  - Session RPM
  - Email signups

### Site Work
- Maintain accurate metadata on all pages (title, description, canonical).
- Keep `sitemap.xml` and `robots.txt` current as pages are added.
- Add/update "last updated" dates consistently on data pages.

### Deliverables
- KPI dashboard template (weekly snapshot).
- Resource hub page live with internal links.

---

## Phase 2 (Weeks 3-4): Content Sprint #1

### Publish Recruiting Articles
- How to Email College Coaches for Recruiting
- College Recruiting Timeline by Grade
- First Follow-Up Email Template
- Common Recruiting Mistakes to Avoid

### Content Rules
- 1 clear search intent per page.
- 800-1500 words for evergreen guides.
- Add internal links to relevant sport and division pages.
- Add a CTA near top and bottom of each article:
  - "Find coach contacts by sport"
  - "Browse Division I/II/III pages"

### Deliverables
- At least 4 indexed recruiting advice pages.
- Internal links from homepage/resource hub to all new articles.

---

## Phase 3 (Weeks 5-6): Division SEO Expansion

### Build Static Division Landing Pages
Start with highest-intent sports:
- Football: D1, D2, D3
- Men's Soccer: D1, D2, D3
- Women's Soccer: D1, D2, D3

### Page Requirements
- Unique title and meta description per division.
- Unique intro copy (150-250 words).
- Division-specific CTA and internal links.
- Same fast table/search experience as master sport pages.

### Deliverables
- 9 static division pages live.
- All included in sitemap and linked from resource hub or parent sport page.

---

## Phase 4 (Weeks 7-8): Distribution Engine

### Weekly Posting Cadence
- 3 short videos/week (TikTok, Reels, Shorts)
- 2 community posts/week (Facebook groups, Reddit where appropriate)
- 1 email newsletter/week

### Content Themes
- Quick recruiting tips
- Coach outreach examples
- "How to use the directory" walkthroughs
- Sport-specific recruiting timelines

### Deliverables
- Repeatable social content calendar.
- First 100+ engaged subscribers target.

---

## Phase 5 (Weeks 9-10): Monetization Improvements

### Ads
- Test ad placements on top landing pages:
  - Above fold
  - In-content
  - Lower content block
- Compare RPM and bounce impact per layout.

### Affiliate + Sponsor
- Add a small "Recommended tools" block to resource pages.
- Draft sponsor one-pager:
  - audience
  - traffic
  - sports covered
  - contact details
- Outreach to at least 20 potential partners.

### Deliverables
- Baseline and improved RPM comparison.
- First sponsor/affiliate conversations started.

---

## Phase 6 (Weeks 11-12): Optimization Loop

### Search Console Optimization
- Identify pages with high impressions and low CTR.
- Rewrite titles/descriptions for those pages.
- Improve internal linking to pages that rank on page 2.

### Conversion Optimization
- Improve newsletter CTA placement and copy.
- Add one downloadable lead magnet:
  - recruiting checklist
  - outreach tracker
  - email templates PDF

### Deliverables
- CTR improvements on priority pages.
- Higher email signup conversion from organic traffic.

---

## Ongoing Weekly Checklist
- Publish 1 new SEO page or article.
- Post 3 short-form videos.
- Share 2 value-first community posts.
- Send 1 newsletter.
- Review metrics for 30 minutes and ship 1 improvement.
