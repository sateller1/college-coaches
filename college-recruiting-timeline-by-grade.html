College Recruiting Timeline by Grade

This timeline keeps families focused on what matters most each year.

Freshman year

  • Build fundamentals and consistent game film.
  • Start a simple athlete resume and track stats.
  • Research schools by academics and athletic fit.

Sophomore year

  • Create a short highlight video and update it seasonally.
  • Start outreach to coaches with brief intro emails.
  • Attend camps or events that fit your target school list.

Junior year

  • Increase outreach cadence and follow up consistently.
  • Refine school list and prioritize communication quality.
  • Prepare for unofficial/official visit conversations.

Senior year

  • Stay organized with deadlines and roster conversations.
  • Keep coaches updated on film, stats, and academics.
  • Finalize fit based on academics, culture, and opportunity.

To build your target list faster, use the resource hub and browse coach directories by sport from the homepage.
