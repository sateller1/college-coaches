Our goal is to save time for student-athletes, parents, and club coaches by offering an easy way to reach out directly to college programs.

How to Use This Directory

Getting started is easy! Each sport page is organized by NCAA division. Simply click one of the Division I, II, or III buttons to load the coaches' contact information for that division. The list includes email addresses, phone numbers, and program details whenever available.

Use the search bar just below the division buttons to quickly find a specific coach, school, or email. The search updates in real time as you type, making it fast and convenient to locate the information you need.

Please note that we work to keep all data as current as possible. Check the date on each sport's page for the most recent update. New sports and programs are added regularly, so be sure to check back often!
