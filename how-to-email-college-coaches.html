How to Email College Coaches for Recruiting

Your first email should be short, specific, and easy for the coach to scan in under 30 seconds.

What to include

  • Graduation year, position/event, and club/high school
  • Why you are interested in that specific program
  • Two to three quick athletic highlights
  • Video link and contact details

Simple first-email template

Subject: 2027 Midfielder from Dallas - Interest in [School Name]
Coach [Last Name],

My name is [Name], and I am a 2027 [Position/Event] at [School/Club]. I am very interested in [School Name] because [specific reason].

A few quick details: [stat/achievement], [stat/achievement], GPA [X.X].
Highlight video: [link]

Thank you for your time.
[Name], [Phone], [Email]
